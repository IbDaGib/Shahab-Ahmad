About Me

Hi, my name is John and I'm a photographer based in New York City. I've been taking photos for over 10 years, and my passion for photography continues to grow every day.

My favorite types of photography are landscape and portrait photography. I love exploring new places and capturing the beauty of the world around us. When I'm not out taking photos, you can usually find me at home editing them.
